Frequently Asked Questions

What You May Want to Know

Are you licensed and insured in Florida?
Yes, All County Construction is fully licensed and insured in the state of Florida, including Orange, Osceola, Seminole, and surrounding counties.
Do you offer free estimates?
Absolutely! We provide free on-site estimates for all projects so you can get a clear understanding of scope and cost before committing.
How long does a typical remodel take?
Project timelines vary by size and scope, but most bathroom remodels take 2–3 weeks, and full kitchen remodels take 4–6 weeks.
What types of remodeling do you specialize in?
We specialize in kitchen and bathroom remodels, home additions, new custom builds, and interior renovations including drywall and painting.
Do you handle permits and inspections?
Yes, we handle all the necessary permits and coordinate with local inspectors to ensure everything is up to code and stress-free for you.
Can I live in my home during the renovation?
In most cases, yes. We do our best to minimize disruption and maintain a clean and safe workspace throughout the process.
What areas do you serve in Central Florida?
We proudly serve Orange, Osceola, Seminole, Lake, Polk, and Volusia counties. If you're nearby, we likely cover your area!
Do you offer financing options?
Yes, we partner with local lenders to offer financing solutions for qualifying clients. Ask us about available plans during your estimate.
Will I get a warranty on the work?
Yes, all work comes with a warranty for both labor and materials. Details depend on the service but typically cover 1–5 years.
How do I get started with a project?
Simply reach out via phone, email, or our contact form. We’ll schedule a free consultation and take it from there!
